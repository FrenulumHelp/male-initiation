treatment - phimosis

First of all, I would prefer my name (email address) to be
kept private...

English Male, 36 yrs old,, obviously un-cut,,

I really don't know how the necrotic process began,,, but
intimacy issues arose literally between my wife and I

flexibility was starting to decrease, bringing first of all
discomfort and then pain,, the pain I fear to say would
over-ride the pleasure of intimacy, so ED arose only when
the head was fully exposed,, otherwise excessive
lubrication had to be used as not to force the skin to
retract,, this theory did work short term for me but not
for my wife who would complain about excessive 'moisture',
thus, this treated the symptom not the cause...

Being an Engineer,,, like all mechanical function,, the
idea of inserting
cylindrical object to stretch it out,,, this I did for what
must seem to be at least half a year,, but it only gave
short term stretch/relief but the continual pain/itching
did not make me a social/sexual creature, plus
the continual adjustment did make me look like I was
infected,, according to my wife I would 'adjust'
automatically without thinking where I was,, so onto the
next step

At its worst, I paid a visit to a Urologist early February
2003 where constriction was about 3/4 Diameter, with
excessive pain to retract, I had the dreaded hour glass
silhouette.

I asked about d-slit, cream or some new procedure, being
cut wasn't preferred but an option he wanted,
at the time I had a rash so he prescribed a steroid cream
0.25 percent which cleaned up the rash (this will become
apparent later) So being cut wasn't what I wanted to hear
so I decided to do some internet search, hence your
website.... thanks

So, the theory of a more aggressive stretching came to
mind,, now for the last 6 weeks,, every time I use the
bathroom I do some therapy,,
started out with being able to insert 2 index finger tips
and manipulate the 'ring"... (when you see stars or your
legs get weak, its time to release) over time the diameter
started to stretch,,, this was more apparent the more
aggressive I got,, the pain I felt I would prefer to be
highly intense for a few minutes each hour rather than
continual aggravation of the previous method. I used a
star pattern (pull and release and then re-clock the
fingers about 60 degrees and start again) ,,, during the
course of the day and assumed 5 Mins. x 12 time a day,, at
the end of the days routine the 'ring' would eventually be
the same diameter of the un-effected skin..

the medication would be applied prior to bed and then the
process of stretching would begin again the next day..
showing a little loss of the previous days gain,, that
being expected,,, skin color changed too,, prior to
stretching the ring was a whitish band,, but the continual
stretching and breaking of the skin would turn a dark
purple/dark red,,, now the skin tone looks normal and
currently doing a more of a maintenance stretch and
application of medication...nominally at 1.25 Diameter...
:)))

Hope this helps,, It may work for some
