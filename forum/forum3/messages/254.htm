a few notes on Stretching the Frenulum

The first possibility is that the frenulum might stretch. I have no
bona fida reports that it has ever worked ... but it is theoretically
possible, because skin stretches, ... we would need a two week
experiment with at least 10 mins a day to find out if this could work
for you ... we would need to plan it, you would need to fill out the
questionnaire to do it scientifically.


Testing this would only be intelligent for someone with a mild degree
of brevity, no blood vessels running through it, and a frenulum which
was not hemmed or topped with a string.

steroids would thin the frenulum skin to help stretching

The only validated reports I have heard are that stretching the
frenulum always rips it. However this could be explained with the idea
that it is a sudden stretch which causes ripping. A gentle regular
stretching is necessary.

(Maybe pulling suddenly could be used to initiate a controlled rip?)

The more often and the more gentle you are the more effect this has
when stretching skin. Repeatedly rhymically tensing and flexing is
more effective than holding a stretch under tension.

The main problem as I see it is stretching/putting the frenulum under
tension is painful - so can you discover a way to stretch without
pain?, maybe even with a little pleasure ...
