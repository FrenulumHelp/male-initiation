PART VI - I give up!

Like I explained in the previous chapter, reaching
goal v0.8 is crucial. In fact, based upon anectodal evidence
of both MYSELF and at least one other regular contributor
on this site, the people who have tried stretching phimosis and
gave up, did so before reaching v0.8. They couldn't get to that point where they could insert two or more fingers into the foreskin, pull it
away from the stomach and stretch comfortably from within for
several hours.

Once you can get there, it's peace of cake! There will be progress
after progress, motivating you to continue all the way to the finish line.

In the beginning, I was NOT able to do that. The only thing i could do
is to grip the tip of the foreskin at the edges and drag them away. Then
I would see a sort of a black tunnel leading to the glans (I didn't see
the glans itself).

So, I thought to myself "I need to stretch this thing from the INSIDE,
but my fingers are too large". I searched for tools I could use, and
suddenly rememered that there existed very handy deviced called....
Q-tips!

Cheap, readily available in almost every grocery store, and with a
protective layer of cotton covering the sharp end (in case something
goes wrong and you poke yourself in the glans), they were simply perfect!

At the beginning, I only inserted two at a time an stretched for hours.
I came to realise that I desperately needed an extra pair of hand that could
operate a THIRD Q-tip. Two Q-tips aren't enough to create a "hole" in the
foreskin tunnel, so that the glans finally becomes visible.

Thought experiment: take a small rubber band and insert two fingers into it. When you try to stretch, in the middle of the band there would be little space. Now try it with 3 or 4 fingers! You can now have a large opening!

Fortunately, it IS possible to operate 3 Q-tips with just two hands. It's
way too difficult to describe it with words, so I won't do it.

Besides, most phimosed people at the very least are able to at least do
SOMETHING - retract the foreskin at least a little bit, and certainly inserting a couple of fingers inside!

Just remember, giving up is very easy, and all this takes time. Stretching
is something that takes hours and months, not minutes and days!
