I was diagnosed with BXO in December after biopsy was done. I had noticed a white, coarse are of skin around my urethral opening which constricted the flow of urine. I had surgery to widen the opening to a normal size and then was given an ointment (Desonide) to apply twice daily for 6 weeks. I just went to the doctor's yesterday and things look much improved. There is very little evidence of BXO and the cream seems to have done the trick (at least for now).
