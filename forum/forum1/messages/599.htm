Hey Nic, something that didn't occur to me when I first wrote you (but it should have) is that sometimes pubic hairs get caught under rolled-up foreskin behind the glans, and they can indeed make things itchy and uncomfortable. If this is what's causing your discomfort, it's very easily solved. Just get some small fingernail scissors or a similar implement, look for hairs that are prone to get entangled under the skin, and carefully trim them off. You might also look at the affected area when it's itching & see if there is any hair trapped under the rolled-up skin. If so, you may be able to solve everything just by pulling the trapped hair out & then checking regularly to make sure it doesn't get caught under there again. It may be lots easier to solve your problem than I first thought. Good luck! Cheers, Bob.
